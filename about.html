My Story (well, the relevent parts at least)

My story starts as many tech professional's does, and is very familure to most young people, fixing a family member's computer system. For me, I helped my grandfather fix his home computer and network. It wasn't as easy as it sounds due to his entire business ran off that small home network. There were many late night/early morning wakup calls when there was a hicqup in the network or his email provider decided to do maintience without notice. The name "AM Security Pro" comes from these experiences.

After my grandfather closed his small business I decided to pursue a career in IT and started a job as a Network Analyst. While there I started to work on my CompTIA Network+ Certification and learn basic scripting with python. With my python program skills, I was able to process spreed sheets with precision and efficency. After a while, I was able to automate much my work with basic python scripting. The Network I helped manage wasn't a typcial BaseT10 or even ethernet based. That said, the basic training I recieved in networking helped me identify similar actions like ARP Request, DoS attacks, and Wireless AP setup. This training enabled me to become one of the top Network Analysts in the company.

While Working as a Network Analyst, I decided to complete my Bachelor's Degree in Information Systems at Northwestern University. While studing, I found myself a new job as a Software Engineer. I took my skills in Python, Networking, Docker, Linux, and more to the next level. I worked on more automation tasks as well as System Administration. I also set myself to collecting my Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) Certification. Inside of a few months, I passed my certifaction and collected my first Certification.
